Welcome to the Demon Slayer Academy

Your journey to becoming a powerful Demon Slayer starts here!

Our Motive for this Academy

Our Motive is to train Demon Slayers to protect humanity from the threat of demons. We aim to provide the best training and resources to our students, ensuring they are well-equipped to face any challenge that comes their way. We believe in the power of teamwork, discipline, and perseverance. Our academy is not just a place to learn; it's a community where students can grow, support each other, and become the best versions of themselves.

Our Principal : Mr. Kagaya Ubuyashiki

“Welcome, brave souls… You stand at the gates of a sacred path — one lined not with comfort, but with courage. This academy is not built on power alone, but on unwavering resolve… and a heart that refuses to abandon the innocent. Here, you will not simply learn to wield a sword — you will learn to protect. You will not merely memorize techniques — you will become the embodiment of them. The demons you face, both outside and within, will test you. But know this — even in darkness, a single flame can light the way. We do not seek perfection. We seek hearts that will rise again… and again… no matter how many times they fall. If your soul burns with purpose, if your spirit does not yield, then step forward… For the path of the Demon Slayer is open to you. And we… will walk it together.”
